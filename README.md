## Week 8 – System Pitch (Team Project)

For the final project in Week 8, our team created a **System Pitch Deck** that presents a redesigned digital experience for National Louis University.

The system focuses on improving the NLU app and website experience for students and professors by creating a **Unified Student Portal**. The goal is to reduce navigation issues, login friction, and support delays.

Key features of the proposed system include:
- A **central dashboard** for quick access to courses and services
- **Guided support tools** to help users resolve issues faster
- A clearer **issue reporting and resolution workflow**
- A unified portal experience across NLU systems

This pitch demonstrates how enterprise technology and a modular architecture can improve the digital experience while integrating with existing university systems.
